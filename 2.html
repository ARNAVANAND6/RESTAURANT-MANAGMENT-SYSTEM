About Us

Our Journey

Founded in 2015, The Golden Fork began with a simple mission: to bring authentic Indian cuisine to food lovers in Delhi NCR. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our founders, passionate about preserving traditional recipes while embracing modern culinary techniques, have created a unique dining experience that celebrates India's rich culinary heritage.

Today, we serve over 500 customers daily, each dish prepared with the same care and attention to detail that defined our first day in business.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To serve authentic Indian cuisine with modern safety standards, ensuring every customer enjoys delicious, safe food in a welcoming environment.

👁️ Vision

To become the most trusted restaurant where every customer gets safe, delicious food tailored to their dietary needs and preferences.

💎 Values

Quality, authenticity, safety, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.
